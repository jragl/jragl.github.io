Competitive Analysis

iCloud

+
Market Leader
Simple, intuitive interface

-
Folder sharing capabilities
Limited integration on non-Apple systems

Opportunities
5GB free storage space is middle ground of competition
$9.99/mo for 2TB

Weaknesses
Selective syncing
No data sharing (links) expiration

Dropbox

+
First to market
Additional space for recruiting new customers

-
Revenue stream dependent on corporate accounts
Numerous data and account breaches reported

Opportunities
Device migration
Intermediate size plan (~50GB) for reduced price

Weaknesses
2GB is smallest amongst competitors for free account
Encryption key stored in the cloud

Google Drive

+
15GB free space most amongst competitors
Simple, intuitive interface

-
Security and privacy concerns
Number of available products creates confusion

Opportunities
Reported spamming as a result of sharing Google Drive
Reduced quality photo and video storage in free plan

Weaknesses
Price structure for paid plans can be very high ($299.99/mo for 30TB)
China market tainting Western perception of Googleâ€™s fairness
